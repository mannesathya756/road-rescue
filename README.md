🚗 Road Rescue

Road Rescue is an on-road vehicle breakdown assistance platform designed to connect stranded drivers with nearby certified mechanics and towing services in real time using GPS tracking and live location updates.

The project aims to provide fast, reliable, and secure roadside assistance through a user-friendly web/mobile application.

📌 Problem Statement

Motorists often struggle to find immediate roadside assistance during vehicle breakdowns, especially in remote or unfamiliar locations. This can lead to:

Long waiting times
Safety risks
Difficulty finding trusted mechanics
Increased stress during emergencies

Road Rescue solves this problem by connecting users with nearby verified service providers instantly.
